# Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## 1. Information We Collect
We collect personal identification information (Name, Email address) when you register on our platform or initiate a transaction. Payment details are processed securely through our Merchant of Record, Paddle, and are not stored on our servers.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use your information to:
- Establish and secure your member account.
- Deliver course materials and sync your curriculum progress.
- Dispatch receipt records and invoice updates.
- Facilitate password recovery.

## 3. Data Protection
We implement industry-standard security measures to safeguard your personal details. All web traffic and Sanctum authentication handshakes are encrypted via SSL.

## 4. Web Cookies
We utilize cookies to maintain session states and verify CSRF protection tokens during your usage of Aether.
